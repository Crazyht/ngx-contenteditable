ngx-contenteditable

It's a very easy way to validate a contenteditable for Angular with ngModel. You can use required="true", minlength="2", maxlength="15" and name="example1" or any custom validator !

First example :

Here, we use a simple 
<p contenteditable [(ngModel)]="text" name="example1" required="true" minlength="2" maxlength="15" #example1="ngModel"></p>
for the first example. Try it !

Example 1 is required
Example 1 need at least 2 characters
Example 1 need maximum 15 characters

Multiline example :

Here, just add multiline="true" to the first example for type on any lines you want by press the touch "Enter". Try it !

Example 2 is required
Example 2 need at least 2 characters
Example 2 need maximum 30 characters
